Fukuyama's Fury is a 3rd-party OpenRA mod based on
the OpenRA Red Alert 2 mod. It was originally hard-
forked from Romanov's Vengeance, another RA2 mod
created by MustaphaTR, which is considerably more 
enjoyable and playable than this one.

This mod uses modifications from the Attacque
SupÃ©rior engine created by Gyula Zimmermann (Graion
Dilach). The creator is very grateful for the powerful
codebase of AS and also highly recommends Valiant 
Shades, a fun RA2-based mod created by Graion that
puts the capabilities of AS to full use. Gratitude
is also hereby given to other contributors to the
AS extension.
